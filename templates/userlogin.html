We are more than just a company

Onestop is India’s leading e-commerce marketplace offering over 30 million products cross 70+ categories including Books, Media, Consumer Electronics and Lifestyle. Onestop is known for its path-breaking services like Cash on Delivery. Onestop is the only online player offering services like In-a-Day Guarantee (50 cities) and Same-Day-Guarantee (13 cities) at scale. Its annual subscription service, onestop First, is the first of its kind in the country. Launched in 2017, onestop has become the preferred online marketplace for leading Indian and international brands. Onestop, currently 33,000 people strong, has 75 million registered users clocking over 10 million daily visits. Onestop's technology has enabled it to deliver 8 million shipments per month – and this number is only growing.
